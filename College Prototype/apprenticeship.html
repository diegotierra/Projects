Apprenticeship Preparation

Applying for an Apprenticeship is a daunting prospect for many young people, as many have never had to write a CV, attend an interview or had a full time job.

We have produced some useful tips to help you when applying for an Apprenticeship.

Apprentice Awards

Every year we like to celebrate our apprentices, their employers and the success stories that have happened over the year at our Annual Awards Ceremony.

Apprenticeship Courses

At Lancaster and Morecambe College we offer a wide range of apprenticeships within different industries.

Traineeships

An opportunity to gain real work experience, job skills and improve your English and maths, if needed. The programme can last up to a maximum of six months with the content tailored to your individual career needs.

Employer Information

LMC offers a wide range of Apprenticeships and work related courses to help your staff develop their skills and increase their impact on your business. Our training consultants are happy to come and talk to you about apprenticeship opportunities.

Offering an apprenticeship to someone is a great way to support individuals to either re-train in a new industry, to start a career or provide new skills to people already working within your business.

Supporting an apprentice will give your business a definite return on investment by boosting the skills of the workforce. Employers who have established apprenticeship programmes reported that productivity in their business improved by 76%.
