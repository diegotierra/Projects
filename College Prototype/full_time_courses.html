Full Time Courses

At Lancaster & Morecambe College we have many courses to choose from across a huge range of subjects, teaching methods and levels. With us, you’ll find the ideal course that’s suitable for you.

We’re one of the top colleges in UK in terms of student satisfaction. We've received recognition for our teaching and Higher Education. This means that when you choose Lancaster & Morecambe College, you can be assured that you will receive an extraordinary level of teaching and support to help you succeed.

With us you’ll have the freedom to choose a subject that interests you, and learn in the way that suits you the best. Many of our courses offer the option of classroom-based study, hands-on practical learning, or on-the-job apprenticeships where you earn while you learn.
