What We Offer?


Opportunities

High quality technical, vocational and professional development opportunities have always been meeting young people, adults and employers.

International Experiences

We have been running European work experience projects for over a decade increasing the personal and professional development of students.

Apprenticeships

Develop your skills, knowledge and behaviours whilst you earn a salary and start your long term career path.

Free Transport

We are offering full-time students (studying 14 hours a week or more) daily return transport on the routes detailed below during term time.











Construction

Whether you choose to specialise in Brickwork, Plumbing or Carpentry and Joinery, there are loads of opportunities including working for local, national and international contractors or becoming your own boss!

Business

All organisations are businesses, so studying Business as an option allows you flexibility across a variety of sectors, whether that’s fund-raising for a local charity or working abroad for a global blue-chip company.

Child Education

You will study a wide range of units that will help prepare you to work in the child education sector. The full time courses include a large proportion of work experience in a variety of placement settings where you will be able to apply theory to your practice.

Creative Digital Media

The Creative Media Industry offers a huge variety of opportunity.Students go on to study and work in interactive media, moving image, 3D visualisation, animation, web-design, games production and design.
